### Golden Rules for Alibaba Sourcing (To Avoid Scams):
1. **Always use "Trade Assurance":** Never pay outside of Alibaba (no Western Union, no direct bank transfers). If you use Trade Assurance and the products are bad, you get your money back.
2. **Filter by "Verified Supplier":** Only talk to suppliers with the blue "Verified" checkmark and at least 3+ years on the platform.
3. **Ask for "DDP Shipping":** DDP stands for "Delivered Duty Paid." This means the supplier handles all Saudi customs, import taxes, and door-to-door delivery. You just pay them once on Alibaba, and the boxes arrive at your door in KSA.
4. **Insist on custom packaging even at low MOQ:** since your raw parts are commodity items, the branded box/insert card IS the product differentiation. Don't skip this — it's what turns a $0.35 part into a 199 SAR kit.
5. **The Script to Copy/Paste to Suppliers:**
   > *"Hello, my name is [Your Name] from WAZZEN KSA. We are an e-commerce brand in Saudi Arabia. We are looking to do a test order of [Quantity] units, bundled into sets with custom branded packaging. We need high quality. Can you please provide your best price for the product + packaging, and your DDP shipping cost by air to Riyadh, Saudi Arabia? If this test goes well, our next order will be 1,000+ units."*
