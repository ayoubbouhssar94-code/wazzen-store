# CRO Page Sections

## Global CRO Principles

- Every page must answer: "Why should I trust this brand today?"
- Lead with pain and emotion, then explain the mechanism, then show proof.
- Make COD visible before the user worries about payment.
- Reassure delivery and WhatsApp/contact support.
- Use sticky mobile CTA on product pages.
- Use alternating desktop sections: text right/image left, then image right/text left.
- Avoid cheap countdown spam. Scarcity must look premium.

## Homepage

Recommended structure:

1. Header.
2. Hero: brand promise + 3-product visual + CTA.
3. Trust strip: COD, fast delivery, warranty/replacement, WhatsApp support.
4. Problem section: direct sun, low tire pressure, windshield chips.
5. Product system section: parked-car protection, roadside readiness, early chip repair.
6. Featured product cards with bundle offers.
7. Science/authority section: reflective layers, pressure measurement, resin injection, Gulf climate.
8. Social proof wall: UGC/review placeholders.
9. Offer section: `اختاري باقتك ووفرّي أكثر`.
10. FAQ objections: delivery, COD, compatibility, returns.
11. Final CTA.
12. Footer.

Hero copy:

```text
وازن سيارتك ضد مفاجآت الطريق وشمس السعودية
حلول مختارة للسائق: حماية من الشمس، ضبط ضغط الإطارات، ومعالجة النقر المناسبة في الزجاج.
اطلبي الآن والدفع عند الاستلام.
```

## Collection Page

Purpose: help visitors compare the 3 products quickly and enter the right PDP.

Sections:

1. Collection hero: `كل حلول وازن في مكان واحد`.
2. Filter-like benefit tabs: شمس، ضغط الإطارات، نقر الزجاج.
3. Three product cards.
4. Bundle education: why 2 or 3 pieces make sense for family/multiple cars.
5. Trust/FAQ strip.

## Product Page Template

Each PDP must include:

1. Above-the-fold product hero:
   - Product image placeholder.
   - Arabic name.
   - Benefit headline.
   - Star rating.
   - Trust badges.
   - Offer selector.
   - CTA: `أضيفي العرض للسلة`.
2. Pain agitation:
   - Describe the daily frustration in Saudi dialect.
3. Mechanism:
   - Explain why this product works.
4. Materials/spec sheet:
   - Premium materials vs cheap alternative.
5. Visual proof section:
   - 3-4 image/video placeholders.
6. Social proof:
   - Review cards and UGC placeholders.
7. Comparison section:
   - WAZZEN vs generic accessory.
8. Cross-sell section:
   - The other two products.
9. FAQ.
10. Sticky mobile CTA.
